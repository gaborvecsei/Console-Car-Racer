# Car Racer

Simple car racing game which you can play in your console!

## Control

| Player | Moving  |
|--------|---------|
| LEFT   | A       |
| RIGHT  | D       |


## How to play

**Player**: *o*

**Other cars (enemies)**: *x*

Try to get out of the way from the other cars and collect as many points as you can!
